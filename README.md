# Reel Joy Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** June 2026

## Who we are

Reel Joy is a personal iOS app developed by Sean Walsh in Brisbane, Australia. The app aggregates publicly available cinema session times from multiple Brisbane cinema chains into a single browsing view. The app links out to each cinema's official booking page and does not handle ticket purchases.

For any questions about this policy, contact: bobaluga69@gmail.com

## What data we collect

**None.** Reel Joy does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information about you. There are no user accounts, no logins, no analytics, and no tracking.

## What is stored on your device

The only data Reel Joy stores is your list of favourite cinemas. This is saved locally on your device using Apple's standard preferences storage and never leaves your phone. Uninstalling the app removes this data.

## Third-party services

Reel Joy fetches publicly available cinema session data from a small set of third-party services. None of your personal data is sent to any of these:

- **Vercel** — hosts the proxy server that aggregates cinema data. https://vercel.com/legal/privacy-policy
- **Supabase** — stores the aggregated cinema and session data. https://supabase.com/privacy
- **The Movie Database (TMDB)** — provides movie posters and metadata. https://www.themoviedb.org/privacy-policy
- **Cinema booking pages** — when you tap a session, Safari opens the cinema's own website. That site's privacy policy applies. Reel Joy is not affiliated with any cinema chain.

## Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will replace this page and the "Last updated" date will be revised.
